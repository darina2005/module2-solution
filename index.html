OUR MENU

JUDUTUThe typical Garifuna food is mainly based on the use of roots and tubers for its preparation. Among them are sweet yucca, bitter yucca, sweet potatoes, taro, yams, also plantains, bananas and coconuts. Among the meats they use are beef, pork and chicken, chickens but also the meat of tepezcuintle, iguana, venison, cusuco, turkeys, raccoons, wild pig and freshwater turtles, saltwater turtle, all this without missing the fruits of the sea; fish, shrimp, snails, crab, crabs, lobsters, turtles, etc.
